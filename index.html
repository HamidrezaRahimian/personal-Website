About

Hi, I’m Alex! I’m a 24-year-old computer science enthusiast, currently pursuing a dual study program in Applied Computer Science (AI) at DHBW Mosbach, Bad Mergentheim campus. With a solid academic foundation, a passion for technology, and a knack for solving complex problems, I’m diving deep into software development, data-driven solutions, and innovative project design.

Born in July 2000, I’ve developed expertise in Java, Kotlin, and web technologies through diverse and challenging projects.   • Personal Growth: Whether it’s learning PHP, experimenting with hair treatments, or pursuing new hobbies, I’m always eager to explore new paths.

When I’m not coding, I love to stay active and adventurous. I’ve run three marathons, climbed Mount Damavand to a height of 5,671 meters, and visited 18 countries in 2024 alone. I’m also an avid badminton player and enjoy exploring ideas for passive income. A fan of sci-fi movies like Inception and Oblivion, I’m constantly learning to balance a busy lifestyle with healthy habits—like finding the perfect pair of white sneakers or planning trips to visit my family in Iran.
